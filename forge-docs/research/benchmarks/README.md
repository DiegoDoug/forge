## 1. What belongs here

- Startup time, bundle size, or query latency measurements for Forge itself, tracked over time.
- Head-to-head comparisons against a specific competitor tool for a specific workflow (e.g. "time to generate and copy a UUID: Forge vs. a browser bookmarklet vs. DevToys").

## 2. What doesn't belong here

- Marketing-style claims without a reproducible method — record the method alongside the number, or don't record the number.
